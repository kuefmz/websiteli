## YouTube Shorts script

More website enquiries are not always better.

Qualified leads are better.

First, give each page one clear goal.

Second, ask only the questions that change what happens next. Service type. Urgency. Location. Budget range. Useful context.

Third, route each enquiry automatically. Urgent leads can alert the team. Standard leads can receive a booking link. Low-fit enquiries can receive a helpful alternative.

Fourth, use AI carefully. It can summarise the message, detect the service type, flag missing details and draft a reply. People should still make the important decisions.

Finally, measure outcomes. Track completed forms, booked calls and qualified opportunities. Do not stop at page views.
